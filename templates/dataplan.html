Game-Onz

What is Game-Onz?

Game-Onz is a prepaid package that gives you:

  • Unlimited gaming data
  • High-speed data
  • Free unlimited* data for music streaming

How to purchase Game-Onz?

There are two ways to purchase Game-Onz:

  • Via the MyUMobile App (available from the App Store, Google Play and App Gallery)
  • Via UMB: Dial *118*3*5# and choose your desired package

How to use Game-Onz?

To use Game-Onz, simply enable mobile hotspot on your mobile phone and tether your laptop or PC, then launch one of the supported games and start playing.
